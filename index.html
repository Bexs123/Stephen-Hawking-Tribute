Plans For A Trip To Space

In late 2006, Hawking revealed in a BBC interview that one of his greatest unfulfilled desires was to travel to space.

Richard Branson offered a free flight into space with Virgin Galactic, which Hawking immediately accepted. On 26 April 2007, Hawking flew aboard a specially-modified Boeing 727-200 jet operated by Zero-G Corp off the coast of Florida to experience weightlessness.

At the time, the date of Hawking's trip to space was projected to be as early as 2009, but commerical flights to space did not commence before his death
